About Me

Welcome to my portfolio. I am Tanaka Ryu, a young digital illustrator specializing in moody and dramatic character portraits. Also accepting Concept Art, Background, and Book Cover Commission. My primary focus is capturing emotion through cinematic lighting and a detailed painterly and semi-realism style. For me, art is not about speed, it is about quality. Each piece you see in this gallery is the result of intense dedication and focus, often requiring 5-10+ hours of pure rendering and refinement. I maintain limited commission slots 3-4 per month to ensure every project receives the attention it deserves. Let's create something remarkable together.
