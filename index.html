The Artist’s Manifesto

About the Vision

Welcome to the digital atelier of Tanaka Ryu. I am a digital illustrator focused on the intersection of cinematic lighting and traditional-style textures. My work revolves around storytelling through atmospheric visuals, specializing in a painterly aesthetic that brings depth and soul to every stroke. I believe that a character is not just a model, but a narrative waiting to be told through light and shadow.

The Expertise

  • Character Design & Portraits: Crafting moody, expressive individuals with a focus on personality.
  • Concept & Background Art: Building immersive worlds with cinematic depth.
  • Book Covers & Illustration: Creating high-impact visuals designed to captivate and engage.
  • Character Models: Detailed references built for consistency and professional use.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay?

I currently accept payments via PayPal, Patreon, or QRIS. Full payment or a 50% down payment is required before I start the rendering process.

Can I use the art for merchandise?

For standard commissions, it's for personal use only. However, the Diamond Tier includes a commercial use license for book covers or merchandise.

Do you draw Furry or NSFW?

No. I do not draw NSFW, Mecha, or Furry (though animal ears/tails on humans are perfectly fine!).

What software do you use?

I use Krita for high-end rendering on desktop and IbisPaint X for mobile sketching and detailing.
